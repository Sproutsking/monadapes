How The Ecosystem Works

1

Mint Your Monad Ape

Purchase your unique Monad Ape NFT during our public mint. Each NFT is your key to the entire ecosystem, granting you access to exclusive features, governance rights, and profit sharing opportunities.

2

Earn $MONAD Tokens

Stake your NFTs to earn $MONAD utility tokens passively. The rarer your NFT, the more tokens you earn. These tokens power the entire ecosystem and can be used for upgrades, governance, and exclusive marketplace purchases.

3

Participate in Governance

Use your NFTs and $MONAD tokens to vote on project decisions through the Monad Empire governance system. Propose new features, vote on partnerships, and shape the future direction of the project.

4

Receive Revenue Share

Automatically receive 60% of all secondary sales royalties distributed proportionally based on your NFT's rarity. The more you hold, the more you earn—creating true passive income from the ecosystem's growth.

5

Access Exclusive Benefits

Shop in the Monad Merch Market with your $MONAD tokens, attend holder-only events, get early access to partner projects, and unlock mystery boxes filled with exclusive utilities and surprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Monad Apes is a revolutionary NFT collection built on the Monad blockchain. It's more than just digital art it's a complete ecosystem featuring a utility token ($MONAD), governance system (Monad Empire), merchandise marketplace, and revenue-sharing model that rewards holders for participating in the community.

Connect your Web3 wallet to our minting platform when the public sale goes live. Make sure you have enough cryptocurrency to cover the mint price plus gas fees. Whitelist members will get early access—check your wallet address above to see if you're whitelisted.

$MONAD is our utility token that powers the ecosystem. You earn it by staking your NFTs the rarer your NFT, the higher your rewards. Use $MONAD for governance voting, trait upgrades, marketplace purchases, and accessing exclusive features within the Monad Apes ecosystem.

We take 10% royalties on all secondary market sales. From this, 60% goes directly to NFT holders (distributed by rarity), 20% to the community treasury, and 20% to development and marketing. This means the more the collection trades, the more passive income you earn.

The Mystery Box is a special reward for dedicated collectors. Mint 10 NFTs to unlock your Mystery Box, which contains exclusive utilities, early access to future drops, special edition merchandise, and other surprises that enhance your Monad Apes experience.

Holders can access the Monad Empire governance portal to vote on proposals, suggest new features, and help decide the project's future direction. Your voting power is determined by the number and rarity of your NFTs, plus your $MONAD token holdings.

We combine multiple value streams: passive income through revenue sharing, active earning through staking, real governance power, exclusive merchandise marketplace, and IRL events. Plus, we're built on Monad—a next-gen blockchain offering superior speed and lower costs than traditional platforms.

Join our vibrant community on Discord for daily discussions, Twitter/X for announcements and alpha, TikTok would follow soon with creative content, and Facebook for broader engagement. Links are available in the footer. Our team is active across all platforms and regularly engages with the community.
