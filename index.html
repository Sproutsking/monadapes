Ecosystem Features

$MONAD Energy Token

$MONAD is the utility token that powers the entire ecosystem.

  • Staking & Reward Distribution
  • Payment in the Monad Merch Market
  • NFT Trait Upgrades / Evolution
  • Governance Voting Power
  • Future project whitelisting

Monad Empire (Governance)

The holder-only governance system that decides the future of the project.

  • Vote on new project developments
  • Submit proposals
  • Participate in funding votes

Monad Merch Market

A high-tech merch marketplace for both digital and IRL products.

  • Holders get early/exclusive access
  • Pay with $MONAD or fiat
  • Limited editions by rarity
  • On-chain QR/NFC authenticity

Holders' Revenue Sharing

10% royalties on all secondary trades are distributed automatically:

  • 60% to holders (by rarity)
  • 20% to treasury
  • 20% to dev & marketing

IRL Connection

Monad Apes bridges digital and physical worlds:

  • On-chain merch tracking
  • Exclusive events for top holders
  • Monad Pop-Up activations
